This model is intended to develop one's understanding of the conservation of momentum. It presents a 1-dimensional collision, either inelactic or fully elastic (as those are the options usually presented to introductory students).

This module is coded in JavaScript, using three.js for the mesh development and the 3D environment. The modeling physics were developed myself while under contract for the University of Louisville.
